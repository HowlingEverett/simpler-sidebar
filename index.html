Simpler-Sidebar

A simple sidebar written in jQuery

Simpler-Sidebar is a fork of Simple-Sidebar

Simple-Sidebar vs Simpler-Sidebar

  1. Stop supporting subwrapper;
  2. Animating only the sidebar and not the entire page;

Why this changes?

  1. You should know how to let the sidebar content overflow (if not check out the set-up tips) so to shrink the code I decided to get rid of this feature;
  2. Animating the entire content was often an issue, position: fixed and position: absolute elements didn't animate the way they should, so I decided to only animate the sidebar;
